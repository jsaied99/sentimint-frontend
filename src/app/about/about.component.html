About

The What

This application allows a user to input a keyword to see a variety of interesting datapoints for that specific word. The user is also able to sign in to see their history of searches.

The Why

Our team wanted to work on a project that was both interesting and gave us experience with machine learning. We decided that giving people the ability to see public perception for different topics was indeed very cool, so the fact that it also gave us a chance to experiment with machine learning made it a no-brainer!
